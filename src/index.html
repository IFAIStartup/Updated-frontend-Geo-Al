Object detection

With Corresponding Coordinates

Object detection in satellite and aerial imagery involves identifying and classifying various objects such as buildings, roads, vegetation, and more. This process uses deep learning models like YOLO (You Only Look Once) to analyze high-resolution images and detect objects. The model not only classifies objects but also returns their precise location within the image in the form of bounding boxes. These boxes contain the pixel coordinates of the detected objects. By georeferencing, the pixel coordinates are mapped to real-world geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude), enabling applications like urban planning, environmental monitoring, and disaster management.

What is GEO AI ?

Geospatial Artificial Intelligence is the application of artificial intelligence (AI) fused with geospatial data, science, and technology to accelerate real-world understanding of business opportunities, environmental impacts, and operational risks. Organizations are modernizing operations to run at scale through automated data generation and approachable spatial tools and algorithms.
